About Avery Analytics

Avery Analytics is a professional bookkeeping and business consulting service designed to help small businesses manage their finances and drive strategic growth. Based in Norfolk, we work with clients remotely nationwide, providing customized solutions for your business needs.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide small business owners with clear financial visibility and strategic business insights that enable informed decision-making and sustainable growth. We combine financial expertise with technology to deliver solutions that transform your business operations.

Our Approach

At Avery Analytics, we believe you shouldn't have to choose between managing your books and growing your business. Our approach eliminates the hassle of bookkeeping while providing you with a strategic business partner focused on your growth:

  • Eliminate Bookkeeping Headaches - We handle the day-to-day transaction management, reconciliation, and reporting so you can focus on your core business
  • Gain Strategic Insights - Beyond basic bookkeeping, we analyze your financial data to identify growth opportunities and efficiency improvements
  • Leverage AI Technology - We use advanced technology to automate repetitive tasks and create custom solutions specific to your business needs
  • Build a Partnership - We work as an extension of your team, providing ongoing support and strategic advice

AI-Powered Business Solutions

What sets us apart is our commitment to leveraging artificial intelligence to develop custom solutions for our clients. Our AI integration allows us to:

  • Automate data entry and categorization with higher accuracy
  • Create custom dashboards and reporting systems tailored to your specific KPIs
  • Develop predictive financial models to forecast cash flow and profitability
  • Implement efficiency-enhancing systems that scale with your business
